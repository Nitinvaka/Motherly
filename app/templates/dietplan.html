🌸 First Trimester Diet Plan

🗓 General Plan

🥣 Focus:

  • Folic Acid (green leafy veggies, fortified cereals)
  • Vitamin B6 (bananas, potatoes)
  • Light small frequent meals to manage nausea
  • Protein-rich foods (chicken, lentils, eggs)

🍴 Example Daily Meals:

  • Breakfast: Vegetable poha + Orange juice
  • Snack: Almonds + Papaya cubes
  • Lunch: Dal Khichdi + Curd + Salad
  • Snack: Sprouts Chaat / Fruit Salad
  • Dinner: Palak Paneer + Roti + Buttermilk
  • Night Snack: Warm milk + 1 Date

🌟 Second Trimester Diet Plan

🗓 General Plan

🥣 Focus:

  • Calcium for baby's bones (Milk, yogurt, cheese)
  • Iron-rich foods to prevent anemia (Spinach, legumes)
  • Protein intake for growing baby tissues
  • Omega-3 fats for brain development (walnuts, chia seeds)

🍴 Example Daily Meals:

  • Breakfast: Vegetable Paratha + Curd
  • Snack: Apple + Peanut Butter
  • Lunch: Rajma + Brown Rice + Salad
  • Snack: Roasted Chana + Buttermilk
  • Dinner: Dal Tadka + Jeera Rice + Veggies
  • Night Snack: Greek Yogurt + Walnuts

👶 Third Trimester Diet Plan

🗓 General Plan

🥣 Focus:

  • Extra calories needed for rapid baby growth
  • Protein-rich foods for tissue and brain development
  • Foods rich in Vitamin K (broccoli, spinach) for blood clotting
  • Small, frequent meals to avoid heartburn
  • Stay hydrated to prevent swelling and constipation

🍴 Example Daily Meals:

  • Breakfast: Oats with nuts + Banana
  • Snack: Handful of mixed seeds (flax, chia, sunflower)
  • Lunch: Grilled Chicken Salad / Paneer Wrap
  • Snack: Smoothie (yogurt + berries)
  • Dinner: Vegetable Soup + 2 Multigrain Chapatis
  • Night Snack: Warm Milk + Dates
